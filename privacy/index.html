Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

This is a placeholder policy that reflects our current practices in plain language. We will replace it with counsel-reviewed terms before any broad launch. Questions in the meantime? Email cmyers@mainandmachine.com.

What we collect

When you book an assessment or contact us, we collect the information you give us — your name, email, phone, company, website, and the details you share about your workflows. We also collect basic, non-identifying analytics about how the site is used.

How we use it

  • To respond to your request and schedule your assessment.
  • To prepare for and follow up on our conversations with you.
  • To improve the site and our services.

How we share it

We do not sell your information. We share it only with the service providers that help us operate — for example, our email delivery provider — and only as needed to do the work you asked for.

Data retention

We keep what you send us for as long as we have an active or prospective relationship, and as needed to meet legal and accounting obligations.

Your choices

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the information we hold about you at any time. Email cmyers@mainandmachine.com and we will take care of it.
