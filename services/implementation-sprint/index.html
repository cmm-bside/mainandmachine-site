What it is

We build inside your real operation, not a sandbox.

Small, working, tested. You watch it run and earn your trust before it touches anything that matters.

A sprint takes the workflows worth automating and builds the thing that fixes them. Agents that draft the repetitive decisions and route the judgment calls back to a person. Automations between the systems you already run. Integrations that pull your data into one source of truth instead of five systems that disagree.

One team runs it from kickoff to handoff. The advisor who scopes your sprint is the one who builds it; no junior team quietly takes over after you sign. Each workflow lands in about 90 days, because a project without a deadline is a project that dies.

And before any of it starts, you get the exact price in writing. It does not move after we start. Handoff means your team trained to run all of it, and ownership of all of it.
