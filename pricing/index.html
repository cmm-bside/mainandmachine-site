01How much does AI implementation cost for a small business?+

The AI Readiness Audit runs $3,500–$8,500 and takes 2 to 4 weeks. An AI Implementation Sprint runs $12,000–$45,000, quoted fixed in writing before work begins. Managed Services is an optional monthly retainer with no lock-in.

02What does the AI Readiness Audit include?+

A workflow map of your real operations, the handful of places AI actually pays, a phased plan you own outright, and a straight read on whether to build at all. It takes 2 to 4 weeks.

03Why fixed prices instead of hourly?+

Because that is how you would want to be treated. The exact price is quoted in writing before work begins and does not move after we start. You will not sit through three discovery calls to learn what something costs.

04What happens after the 90 days?+

Your team is trained to run what we built, and you own all of it. If you want us to keep watching, Managed Services covers monitoring and maintenance as models and vendors shift — a monthly retainer you can cancel any month it stops earning its keep.
