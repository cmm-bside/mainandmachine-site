01What exactly is the assessment?+

A free 30-minute working session with a senior advisor. We walk your workflows: where the time goes, what it costs, what a machine could carry. You leave with a plain-language read on where AI pays in your business and a rough dollar range. It ends there unless you want more. We reply within 24 hours to set a time.

02Is it really free?+

Yes. No card, no catch. 30 minutes with a senior advisor, a clear read on your business, and that’s it. We do this because the businesses worth building with tend to start here.

03What if AI isn’t right for my business?+

Then we’ll tell you. Sometimes the answer is “wait a quarter,” or “you don’t need us for this.” You’ll leave with 30 minutes back and a clear answer, which is more than most firms hand out.

04Do I need to prepare anything?+

No. Come as you are. It helps if you can speak to how the work moves day to day: where the time goes, where things pile up. That’s all the prep there is.

05Who actually shows up to the call?+

A senior advisor, the person who would scope and build your project, not a junior rep reading off a script.

06What happens to the details I submit?+

They go to us so we can prepare for your call. That’s all. Your details never enter a marketing list, and if we never work together they go nowhere at all.
